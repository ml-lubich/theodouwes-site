# Requirements

## User stories

1. As a visitor, I see Theo’s name as the dominant brand signal in the first viewport.
2. As a visitor, I understand his focus: quantifying developer productivity into business outcomes.
3. As a visitor, I can scan experience (Navigara first) and education (UC Berkeley).
4. As a visitor, I can open featured press and writing links.
5. As a visitor, I can reach LinkedIn from the hero CTA and footer.

## Hard constraints

- Next.js + Bun
- Unit tests, integration tests, and Playwright e2e tests
- Deployable to Vercel
- Mobile and desktop readable layouts
- No hero cards, no dashboard clutter

## Non-goals (v1)

- Blog engine / CMS
- Contact form backend
- Analytics dashboards
- Auth
